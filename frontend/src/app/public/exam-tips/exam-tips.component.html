Exam Tips & Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to ensure a smooth online examination experience

📋 Before the Exam

  • Test your internet connection speed
  • Close all unnecessary browser tabs and applications
  • Have your Student ID ready for verification
  • Login to the system 15 minutes before exam start time
  • Read all exam instructions carefully
  • Ensure your device is fully charged or plugged in
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies

⏱️ During the Exam

  • Manage your time wisely — check the timer regularly
  • Answer easier questions first to build confidence
  • Don't refresh or close the browser window
  • Review all answers before final submission
  • Stay calm and focused throughout the exam
  • Read each question carefully before answering

💻 Technical Tips

  • Use a reliable and stable internet connection
  • Keep your device charged or connected to power
  • Use the latest version of your web browser
  • Disable browser extensions that might interfere
  • Have a backup device ready if possible
  • Know the support contact number for emergencies

⚠️ Important Reminders

If you experience technical difficulties during the exam, contact support immediately at +251-900469816 or debremarkos@dmu.edu.et. Do not attempt to restart the exam without authorization. The system automatically saves your progress.

🚫 What NOT to Do

  • Don't use multiple devices simultaneously
  • Don't share your login credentials
  • Don't open multiple browser windows
  • Don't use unauthorized materials or resources
  • Don't communicate with others during the exam
  • Don't take screenshots of exam questions

✅ After the Exam

  • Confirm that your exam was submitted successfully
  • Take note of the submission confirmation message
  • Wait for results to be published
  • Review your performance when results are available
  • Contact your instructor if you have concerns

🎯 Success Strategies

  • Practice with sample exams if available
  • Familiarize yourself with the exam interface
  • Create a quiet, distraction-free environment
  • Get adequate rest before the exam
  • Stay hydrated and have water nearby
  • Trust your preparation and stay confident
